FLOWERBOY

a conversation with Jared Carmichael

Jared I’m going to say the cockiness of where this flower blooms... It was like... Do you feel like you were accepting yourself?


Tyler Well, sometimes I’m just like. It’s a lot of things that- and I don’t want to take credit but there’s a lot of things that I was doing... and people were like “what was he doing?” and two years later it’s what everyones doing.


Jared mhm.


Tyler Whether it’s the way the videos are doing or these stupid pop shop things or whatever... and im not taking credit but it’s like I was doing that and no ones ever puts me on any of these lists or even respects me in the sense that I want it... So i was just- and that’s why that line is like... “ aint nobody fucking with T, but that may just be my ego, but dont be too cool, because I may freeze.” and I’m stuck in this egotistical mindstate and I never move foward. and that’s how niggas freeze to death but...


Jared But then you carry that into- the ice-


Tyler But that ice shit ways a ton. My ice cause it was cold. Water your garden my nigga and then stunt. I got a flower chain made... like with all these different things and that’s what I was talking about, but I also was like you know what? fuck these niggas. Keep doing my shit. Uh, tell these black kids they can do whatever they want.


Jared That’s one of my favorite uhhh lyrics. Is just tell these black kids they can be who they are.


Tyler Dude right?!


Jared Like it’s one of my favorite- You had another uh... um.. god... you had another lyric like that on cherry bomb. That was just like uh... the world is yours my lil nigga. The world is yours. Like I love that, like it’s a similar thing to tell these black kids they can be who they are. Were you saying that to you? You saying that too?


Tyler Well I was saying that too me too, but like it’s a uh... a uh... It’s, no one saying that to these kids that look like me. To anyone that looks like me. We had this conversation just wanting this snowboard when I was 12 but someone saying “that’s white people shit. Black people don’t do that...


Jared yea...


Tyler no... you don’t do that. Don’t put a damper on me and tell me what I can and can’t be just because of... the way everyone else was thats my same color. And I just feel like.


Jared You are like th-


Tyler I just wanted to tell them like, no. Be you. Be...

Jared Who you are is such the alternative to what expectations are place on young black guy growing up. Like it’s always the- It’s you’re influenced by kinda everything. Just kinda like all cultures, so do you think you accept that about yourself? Through you’re first few albums?


Tyler Oh, I knew who I was at 12.
